Usage¶

Create Account¶

Visit the following link and create your account under the register now button. When signing in, use your ZIH username and @tu-dresden.de email. You will have to wait for the administrator to allow you to log in for the first time, as you need to be verified by the admin. On the first login, you won’t have access to any repositories. Please ask your contact personal or admin for access. .. code-block:: bash

http://141.30.80.100:8080/

or via ssh access

ssh://git@141.30.80.100:7999/Group/SubGroup/repo.git
